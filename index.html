Recipe

Chocolate cake



"This chocolate cake recipe is a classic. Here is fast and easy version of the classic Chocolate Cake recipe. Enjoy!"


Difficulty Preparation Cooking Total time
Easy 10 min 25 min 35 min

Ingridients

Black chocolate: 200g

Sugar: 150g

Butter: 200g

Eggs: 4

Flour: 80g

Instructions

1

Melt chocolate in milk

2

Add butter

3

Add sugar

4

Stear till sugar melts

5

Add flour

6

Add eggs

7

Stear til it's consistent mass

8

Put cake mass into tray

9

Bake for 30 minuts in 250 C
